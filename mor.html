6 – Iraqi Kurdistan visas can’t be used to visit the Arab Iraq – This is important. A valid visa for traveling to Iraqi Kurdistan doesn’t allow you to visit outside of Kurdistan, that is to enter actual Iraq. If you try to enter, you will just bump into very strict military control who will send you back home. Just to clarify, because many people ask me, this also includes Mosul.
